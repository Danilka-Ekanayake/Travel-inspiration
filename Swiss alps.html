Welcome to
Switzerland

Get a mountain and adventure experience

Explore the best 10 places in Switzerland

Zermatt & Matterhorn

Iconic mountain, famous for skiing, mountaineering, and photography. Car-free village with luxury hotels and chalets.

Jungfraujoch – “Top of Europe”

High-altitude railway station and observatory. Snow, glaciers, and panoramic mountain views.

Interlaken

Adventure hub: paragliding, skydiving, hiking. Scenic town between Lake Thun & Lake Brienz.

Grindelwald

Popular for hiking, skiing, and alpine scenery. Base for Eiger mountain excursions.

St. Moritz

Luxury ski resort, famous for winter sports and high-end tourism. Host of winter Olympics & international events.

Lucerne & Mount Pilatus - Rigi

Charming city + nearby mountains. Famous for cable cars, cogwheel trains, and lake cruises.

Saas-Fee

Car-free village, skiing and glacier hiking. Known as “Pearl of the Alps.”

Davos

Skiing and snowboarding paradise. Host of the World Economic Forum.

Aletsch Glacier

Largest glacier in the Alps, UNESCO World Heritage Site. Amazing hiking trails & viewpoints.

Bernese Oberland

Region including Lauterbrunnen, Wengen, and Mürren. Famous waterfalls, alpine valleys, and hiking trails.
