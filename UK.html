Explore the best 10 places in United Kingdom

Sigiriya Rock (Lion Rock)

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Ancient rock fortress with frescoes and panoramic views.

Temple of the Tooth – Kandy

Sacred Buddhist temple housing the tooth relic of Lord Buddha. Cultural and religious hub.

Galle Fort – Galle

Dutch colonial fort and UNESCO site. Seaside views, cafes, and boutique shops.

Nine Arches Bridge – Ella

Famous railway bridge surrounded by tea plantations. Instagram hotspot.

Nuwara Eliya (Hill Country)

Tea plantations, Gregory Lake, colonial architecture. “Little England” of Sri Lanka.

Yala National Park

Safari park famous for leopards, elephants, and wildlife.

Adam’s Peak (Sri Pada)

Pilgrimage site for multiple religions. Famous sunrise hike.

Dambulla Cave Temple

UNESCO site with ancient Buddha statues and murals.

Polonnaruwa Ancient City

Another UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient city ruins, temples, and statues.

Southern Beaches

Unawatuna, Mirissa, Bentota – Popular for relaxation, water sports, and whale watching.
